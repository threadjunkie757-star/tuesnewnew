Frequently Asked Questions

There is no strict minimum — we work with orders of all sizes. However, pricing per unit decreases significantly at higher quantities (25+, 50+, 100+). If you need fewer than 10 pieces, reach out and we will find the best solution for your budget.
Standard turnaround is 7–10 business days from artwork approval. Express 3–5 business days is available for most orders — just mention it when requesting your quote. Same-week rush may be available depending on current capacity.
We prefer vector files (AI, EPS, SVG). We also accept high-resolution PNG or PDF (300 DPI+). Don’t have a print-ready file? Our free design service will recreate or clean up your logo at no charge.
Absolutely. Logo creation and design assistance is included at no charge with every order. Whether you have a rough sketch, a low-res image, or just an idea — our team will build a print-ready design for you before production begins.
We offer embroidery, screen printed apparel, DTF transfers, and custom patches. The right method depends on your garment type, design complexity, and quantity. We will recommend the best option for your order.
Free local delivery throughout Hampton Roads: Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Chesapeake, Suffolk, Hampton, Newport News, and Portsmouth. We also ship anywhere in the US — contact us for shipping rates on out-of-area orders.
We source from 50+ leading brands including Nike, Carhartt, The North Face, Champion, Bella+Canvas, New Era, Brooks Brothers, Eddie Bauer, TravisMathew, Port Authority, Adidas, Under Armour, Columbia, and many more. If you have a specific brand in mind, ask — we can usually source it.
Pricing depends on the garment, quantity, number of print locations, and decoration method. As a general guide: custom t-shirts start around $12+, hats from $10+, and outerwear from $18+. Pricing per unit drops significantly at higher quantities. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote — we'll give you exact pricing based on your specific order.
Yes — we ship anywhere in the United States. Free local delivery applies throughout Hampton Roads (Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Chesapeake, Suffolk, Hampton, Newport News, and Portsmouth). For out-of-area orders, we ship via UPS or FedEx — contact us for shipping rates and options.
